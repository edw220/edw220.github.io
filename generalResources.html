Livesplit

Livesplit is timing software for tracking the length of your run. There are a bunch of useful ways to take advantage of livesplit, including individual stage or even room timers. Additionally, some communities have developed automatic splitting plugins for specific games, but this is limited to games played on PC only. Livesplit is available for download here.

Open Broadcast Software (OBS)

Open Broadcast Software, commonly referred to as OBS, is video capture software often utilized to record speedruns. You can use it to capture gameplay, livesplit, and even inputs. Recording your own gameplay is essential for leaderboard submission, but also has the benefit of being able to review your gameplay for improvement. You can download it here.

Discord

Discord is a fantastic tool to connect with other speedrunners and exchange tips. Additionally, you'll find a number of discord communities (links to some here) that are full of helpful people. You may want to join in on community races, or simply follow along with new tech as it is discovered. Whatever your needs may be, Discord serves to be an excelllent option to network and make friends in a given speedrun community. Click the following link to download Discord.

Speedrun.com (SRC)

Speedrun.com is one of the primary locations where speedruns are submitted, verified, and organized. As a result, this is a great resource to leverage for reviewing current strategies or runs of your peers. At SRC you will be able to see a number of categories for games, as well as their community decided rulesets. Click here to visit Speedrun.com.
