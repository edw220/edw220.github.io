What Is Speedrunning?

Speedrunning is a way to play video games where the “runner” attempts to go as quickly as they can to a target point while staying within community determined rules.

Why Do People Speedrun?

Speedrunning has many functions. It entertains the runners as well as viewers. It also has a deeper meaning of self-improvement. The idea of overcoming adversity to achieve self-set goals. It helps to empower individuals in a greater sense as well as providing a friendly community amongst runners. Check out my page of general resources for more info on how to start!

About Me

I've been a speedrunner for 6 years with a focus primarily on platformers. I've held a couple of world records in the past. I also have a passion for teaching people and sharing my knowledge as well as learning. On that note, don't be shy to reach out if you think you've got a great strategy! One of the biggest misconceptions about speedrunners is they have nothing to learn from casual players. Often we get new strategies from those who have a fresh look at the game and how to play it.
